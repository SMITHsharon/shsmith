Founding Member
Germantown Commons of Tennesseer

I am a founding member of the first successfully-built cohousing community in Tennessee. Cohousing communities are innovative and intentional communities that require out-of-the-box visioning, financing, and management. Acting as developer for the project, our LLC worked directly with our architect on the site design, with the bank on securing the $5,000,000 construction loan, and with our builder through the entire construction and close-out phase of the 25-condo and common property project.

I have held numerous positions for this complex project through the years, including serving on the LLC Managing Board, HOA Board, Finance and Legal Committee, Process Committee, Steering Committee, Membership and Marketing Committee, and others.

Administrative Assistant
Nashville Organized for Action and Hope (NOAH)

Nashville Organized for Action and Hope (NOAH) is a faith-led coalition that is multi-racial and interdenominational comprised of congregations, community organizations, and labor unions that work to give voice to traditionally marginalized people. NOAH engages ordinary people in the political and economic decisions affecting their lives, acting as a unified voice for the faith and justice community to act on its values in the public arena. NOAH's key focus issues are: affordable housing, economic equity, and criminal justice.
