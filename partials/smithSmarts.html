    About NSS

    Nashville Software School is a Tennessee non-profit corporation whose mission is to expand the technology workforce in middle Tennessee by providing motivated individuals with access to a career in software development through training, mentorship and apprenticeship. NSS provides intensive training, mentorship and apprenticeship in order to prepare people with limited or no previous technical training or experience for an entry level software developer job creating web sites, applications and/or mobile apps.

    I completed one year intensive software bootcamp emphasizing full-stack development and technologies including JavaScript, jQuery, AngularJS, and C#. Developed and deployed individual and group apps and interactive software emphasizing industry best practices.
